About me

I hold a Master's in Sociology and am currently pursuing an

MSc in Political Science at Malmö University

I am a dedicated sociologist with a passion for exploring gender studies, social movements, cultures, and social policy. My academic journey is marked by excellence, having graduated first in my class for both my BA and MSc in Sociology at the University of Crete, where I was also awarded multiple scholarships for my research contributions.

My research experience spans qualitative studies, data analysis, and fieldwork across various projects, including work with the Regional Observatory of Social Inclusion. I have presented my findings at several conferences, such as the 26th Panhellenic Postgraduate Intensive Seminar, where I discussed the gender aspects of Greek political graffiti.

In addition to my academic and research endeavors, I co-founded the first student-run radio station at the University of Crete and have diverse work experience in customer service roles. My love for broadcasting, traveling, and exploring different cultures enriches both my personal and professional life.
